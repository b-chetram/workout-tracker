Plateau Protocol
Just grinding the same failed weight over and over is not a plan bro. Here's the actual system.
1
Check Your Life First
Bad sleep? Diet slipping? Weed or drinking? That's your answer before you change anything in the program.
2
Drop Reps, Keep Weight
Instead of 3×5, do 3×3 with the same weight. Get comfortable owning it. Next session go back to full reps.
3
Deload Week — Every 4th Week
Drop to 60% of working weight. Same reps, same exercises. Let your joints and CNS recover. Come back week 5 and you'll break through.
4
Strip Back 10% and Rebuild
Stuck 3+ sessions after the above? Drop 10%, rebuild with perfect form for 2 weeks. You'll fly past your old max.
5
Wave Loading
Instead of adding weight — add a set. 3×5 → 4×5 → 5×5, then add weight and drop back to 3×5. This is real long-term progression.
📅 Deload Schedule
Week 1–3: Push hard, add weight or reps.
Week 4: DELOAD — 60% weight, same reps.
Week 5: Come back fresh and break records.

Non-negotiable. This IS the system.
