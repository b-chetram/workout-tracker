Plateau Protocol
📚 Science Based Approach
Based on Jeff Nippard's evidence based progressive overload principles and Dr. Mike Israetel's autoregulation methodology.
1
Check Your Life First
Sleep, nutrition, stress, hydration? Fix those before touching the program. 90% of plateaus live here.
2
Micro Rep Progression
Got 12/12/10? Don't repeat the same thing. Next session, aim for 12/12/11. Beat your weakest set by just 1 rep. That IS progressive overload.
3
Tempo Manipulation
Same weight, same reps, but add a 3 second eccentric (negative). More time under tension = more growth stimulus without adding load.
4
Swap the Variation
Stuck on flat DB bench? Switch to incline for 3 to 4 weeks. Fresh stimulus on the same muscle group can break through plateaus.
5
Strategic Deload
Drop to 50 to 60% working weight for one week. Same reps, same exercises. Come back the following week stronger. The app tracks your deload timing.
6
Strip 10% and Rebuild
Still stuck after deload? Drop 10% off working weight, rebuild with perfect form and controlled eccentrics for 2 weeks.
7
Add a Set (Wave Loading)
Instead of adding weight, add volume. 3×8 → 4×8 → 5×8, then add weight and reset to 3×8. This is Nippard's preferred approach for accessories.
📅 Deload Schedule
Push hard for 3 to 5 weeks (based on your feedback), then deload. The app recommends based on your session count, but you can postpone if you're feeling good. Listen to your body.
