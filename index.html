Plateau Protocol
Grinding the same failed weight is not a plan. Here's the actual system.
1
Check Your Life First
Sleep, diet, weed, drinking? Fix those before touching the program. 90% of plateaus live here.
2
Drop Reps, Keep Weight
Do 3×3 instead of 3×5 with the same weight. Own it. Next session go back to full reps.
3
Deload Week — Every 4th Week
Drop to 60% working weight. Same reps, same exercises. Come back week 5 stronger.
4
Strip 10% and Rebuild
Still stuck? Drop 10%, rebuild with perfect form for 2 weeks. You'll fly past your old max.
5
Wave Loading
Add a set instead of weight. 3×5 → 4×5 → 5×5, then add weight and reset to 3×5.
📅 Deload Schedule
Weeks 1–3: Push hard.
Week 4: DELOAD — 60% weight, same reps.
Week 5: Break records.

Non-negotiable. This IS the system.
