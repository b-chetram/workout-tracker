Plateau Protocol
Grinding the same failed weight over and over isn't a plan. Here's the actual system.
1
Check Your Life First
Bad sleep, diet slipping, weed, drinking? Fix those before touching the program. Seriously.
2
Drop Reps, Keep Weight
Instead of 3×5 do 3×3 with the same weight. Own it. Next session go back to full reps.
3
Deload Week — Every 4th Week
Drop to 60% of working weight. Same reps, same exercises. Come back week 5 and you'll break through.
4
Strip 10% and Rebuild
Stuck 3+ sessions after all of the above? Drop 10%, rebuild with perfect form. You'll fly past your old max.
5
Wave Loading
Add a set instead of weight. 3×5 → 4×5 → 5×5, then add weight and drop back to 3×5. Real long-term progression.
📅 Deload Schedule
Weeks 1–3: Push hard.
Week 4: DELOAD — 60% weight, same reps.
Week 5: Come back and break records.

Non-negotiable. This IS the system.
